AdEngine module

See the market before you build.

Research gathers visible competitor activity into one place so you can see what is already being tested: hooks, offers, landing-page angles, repeated claims and long-running ad patterns. The point is not to copy competitors. It is to avoid starting blind.

Separate noise from proven patterns.

Winners helps identify the campaigns that look more likely to be working: ads that have stayed live, creatives that appear repeatedly, offers that keep resurfacing, and angles that competitors seem to rely on. This gives you a shortlist of ideas worth studying before creating your own campaign.

Understand each competitor at brand level.

Advertiser Intelligence groups activity by advertiser, domain and ad record so you can see who is active, what they are pushing, what calls to action they repeat, and how their campaign structure changes across different ads.

Turn research into campaign-ready creative.

Create helps move from saved campaign patterns into new hooks, copy angles, creative prompts and draft concepts that your team can review before anything goes live.

Keep useful campaign outputs visible.

Results gives reviewed ideas, saved outputs and shortlisted campaign material a practical place to be checked, filtered and moved forward instead of disappearing after one session.

Keep campaign intelligence organised.

Library gives approved ideas, saved outputs, campaign references and reusable creative material a central place to live, so useful work is not lost between research sessions, documents and browser tabs.

Rebuild the structure, not the asset.

Clone takes the useful structure of a winning advert and turns it into a fresh, brand-safe creative direction. It is designed to preserve the strategic idea while changing the actual creative, wording, composition and visual treatment.

Turn approved creative into usable social output.

Socials converts selected creative into platform-ready post copy, titles, hashtags and short campaign snippets. It helps move from advert concept to usable campaign material without rewriting everything manually.

Move from campaign idea to video workflow.

Video Gen supports a full production path: concept, storyboard, scene visuals, generated clips and final export. Show all four images as a small gallery inside the modal so users understand the workflow is multi-stage, not just a single video button.
