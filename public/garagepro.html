How It Works

From first enquiry to owned action.

GaragePro turns calls, missed enquiries and booking requests into clean records, provisional bookings, reminders and follow-up actions.

1

Customer enquiry

Customer call, missed call or web enquiry arrives.

2

GaragePro captures the job

Vehicle, service type, contact details and preferred time are collected.

3

Booking or callback created

The system creates a provisional booking, confirmed job or callback record.

4

Reminder / follow-up starts

SMS confirmations, MOT reminders or service follow-up can be triggered.

5

Workshop acts from the queue

The owner or team works from one clean dashboard instead of scattered calls and notes.
