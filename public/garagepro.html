Delivery Model

Managed service now, custom operating system when ready.

Frontline AI keeps product delivery controlled: narrow first workflow, clean review, testable changes and practical handover.

Managed Service

Start with a supported monthly setup for the highest-value enquiry, booking or callback workflow.

  • Configured forms, queues and follow-up wording
  • Cloud visibility for records and activity
  • Monthly improvement and support rhythm
  • Controlled changes without a large first build

Custom Build

Expand GaragePro into a deeper workflow platform around your teams, rules and reporting needs.

  • Custom routing, dashboards and records
  • Workflow automation across email, SMS or dashboards
  • Role-based views and handover points
  • Tested release process and documented change control
