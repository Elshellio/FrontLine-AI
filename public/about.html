Why we build this way.

AI systems can become messy quickly if they are rushed. Frontline AI uses controlled delivery so each improvement has purpose, value, review, testing and a stable checkpoint.

InspectWe check the current state before changing anything.
DefineWe agree what the change is meant to achieve.
BuildWe keep work narrow enough to review properly.
TestWe check the route, form, page or AI behaviour.
AcceptApproved work becomes a stable point for future changes.
