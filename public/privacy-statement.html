Privacy Statement

How Frontline AI handles website and enquiry data.

This page explains, in plain English, what information may be collected through the Frontline AI website and how it is used to respond, operate the service and keep the site secure.

This is a placeholder privacy statement for client review. It should be professionally reviewed before final legal publication.

1. Data we may collect

Frontline AI may collect information you choose to submit, plus limited technical information needed to operate the website and services.

  • Book Fact-Find form details, such as name, business, email, phone, preferred contact method, business size, product interest and booking information.
  • Website enquiry details submitted through forms, messages or calls to action.
  • Assistant or chat interactions, including the question asked and the controlled response route used.
  • Technical logs, such as request time, page or endpoint requested, browser details, IP address, errors and security events.

2. How we use the data

We use data to respond to enquiries, book calls, understand what kind of AI worker or workflow may be useful, operate the website, improve services and protect the site from misuse.

Assistant and form interactions may also help identify common questions, content gaps and workflow improvements, especially where a controlled assistant needs better approved material.

3. We do not sell data

Frontline AI does not sell personal data. Information is used for service, enquiry, booking, support, security and improvement purposes.

If trusted suppliers are used for hosting, email, booking, analytics or similar operations, they should only process data for the service purpose they provide.

5. How long data is kept

Data should only be kept for as long as needed for enquiries, booking records, project discussions, service improvement, legal obligations, security logs or legitimate business administration.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the reason it was collected.
